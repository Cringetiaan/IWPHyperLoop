There are a couple of magnetic blocks lying around that you can use to get to tricky places!
If you press 'E' you can swap your polarity. Red means you are pulled towards the block, blue forced away.
You can see the area of effect the blocks have by the transparent orbs around them.
It is your convenient Magnetic Robo Vision that you have for some reason.
Don't ask me why you have it I am not the one that designed you!
