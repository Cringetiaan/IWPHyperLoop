The tube serves as the outer shell of the Hyperloop system, housing both the rails and passenger pods inside it.
By maintaining a vacuum within it, air resistance is eliminated, allowing the pods to travel at extremely high speeds.
The tube itself is made of solid steel, specifically engineered to withstand the strong forces generated by the movement of the pods.
This provides a safe and controlled environment for passenger and cargo transportation.
(Note: The tube is being depicted as transparent in the interest of visual clarity, but the real deal is a closed system that cannot be seen through.)
