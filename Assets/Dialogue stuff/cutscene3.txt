The pods lie at the center of Hyperloop's passenger experience.
Current designs see them being around 2.23m in diameter, their interiors offering room comparable to that of modern passenger trains.
Engineered for safety and comfort alike, they provide a smooth traveling experience - all while reaching top speeds of up to 1220 km/h.
Even then, the maximum innertial acceleration passengers are set to experience is still comparable to that of a commercial airliner.
